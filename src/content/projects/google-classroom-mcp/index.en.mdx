### 💡 Motivation

Managing Google Classroom through the web interface is slow and repetitive: creating assignments, moving them to topics, posting announcements, reviewing submissions... all operations that consume time that could be spent with students. The goal was to be able to do everything with natural language, directly from an AI assistant.

### ✨ 43 Implemented Tools

The server covers the full range of Classroom operations:

* **Courses**: create, update, delete, list and search courses.
* **Students and teachers**: add, remove and list members.
* **Assignments and materials**: create, edit, publish and delete.
* **Topics**: organise course content by subject.
* **Submissions**: consult, grade and return assignments.
* **Rubrics**: create and manage assessment criteria.
* **Announcements**: post and manage course communications.
* **Guardians**: invite and manage students' legal guardians.

### 🤖 Integration with Personal Assistant

This MCP is the central piece of a personal teaching assistant built around Claude Code. Combined with **Google Workspace MCP** (Gmail, Drive, Sheets, Docs, Calendar), it enables the automation of complete workflows:

### 🚀 Impact

* **76 students** managed across 4 courses from a single conversational interface.
* Operations that required 10–15 clicks are done with a single instruction.
* Foundation for a growing ecosystem of teaching automation.
